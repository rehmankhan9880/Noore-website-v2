About Nooré

Nooré is a premium Abaya brand founded by Rehman Khan, blending timeless tradition with modern elegance. Our designs reflect modesty, grace, and confidence — crafted for women who want to express their identity with style and sophistication. Each piece is created with love and care, inspired by classic silhouettes and contemporary colors. Nooré is more than clothing — it's a celebration of culture and beauty.
